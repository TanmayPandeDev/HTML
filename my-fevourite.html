RRR

"RRR," a 2022 Indian Telugu-language epic historical action drama film, is set in pre-independent India and tells the story of two fictional revolutionaries, Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju, who come together to fight against British colonial rule. Directed by S. S. Rajamouli, the film stars N. T. Rama Rao Jr. as Bheem and Ram Charan as Raju. Key Information about RRR: Title: RRR (Roudram Ranam Rudhiram). Director: S. S. Rajamouli. Cast: N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, Ajay Devgn, and others. Genre: Epic historical action drama. Setting: Pre-independent India. Plot: The film follows the fictionalized stories of Komaram Bheem and Alluri Sitarama Raju, two freedom fighters who unite to fight against British oppression. Themes: Patriotism, revolution, friendship, and social justice.
